Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Simple Clock is a simple clock and date display app for Android. This policy explains what data the app collects — and what it does not.

Data Collection

Simple Clock does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no network access.

Permissions

The app requests no special Android permissions.

Third-Party Services

Simple Clock does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
